About Me

Hello and welcome — I’m glad you’re here. My name is Jessica and I work at the intersection of law, business operations and technology. As a legal professional (LL.M.) with a background in business economics, I bring more than 10 years of experience in the life sciences industry, where I worked in highly regulated and complex matrix organisations with a strong hands-on mentality. Along the way, I discovered my passion for backend engineering.

In my experience, the most challenging task is finding the right balance between regulation and innovation. My heart truly beats for both sides: understanding business realities while building reliable compliance and governance frameworks that create trust in technology.

Today I specialize in building operating models and performance frameworks that ensure compliance. My work centers on the practical side of Technical AI Governance, specifically the implementation of the EU AI Act and ISO 42001 standards. I create the structures needed to make AI systems transparent, reliable, and legally sound. My goal is to integrate Responsible AI into daily business operations in a way that builds deep trust in the technology while maintaining the agility a company needs to compete.
